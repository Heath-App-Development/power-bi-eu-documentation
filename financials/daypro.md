## Calculations

**ALL CALCULATIONS REFLECT THE NUMBERS IN COLUMNS C & D ONLY \(Revenue & Expense\)**

### Revenue

Sum of all amounts in column C \(Revenue\)

### Expense

Sum of all amounts in column D \(Expense\)

### Contribution

Revenue - Expense

### Contribution Margin

\(Contribution divided by Revenue\) x 100
